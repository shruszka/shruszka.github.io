What I Do

I bring ideas to life—from the first sketch to a fully realized product, animation, or space. My work blends creative design and technical know-how, covering everything you see below.

Industrial & Product Design

I design physical products people love to use. My process covers everything from the big idea and initial sketches to detailed 3D models ready for manufacturing.

3D Visualization & Motion Graphics

I turn digital models into stunning, photorealistic images and captivating animations—perfect for showcasing a product before it even exists.

Interior & Architectural Design

I design and visualize interior spaces. Whether it's a home or commercial area, I create detailed 3D walkthroughs that make you feel like you're already there.

Graphic & Web Design

Your brand's story, told through visuals. I design memorable logos, create striking graphics, and build clean, modern websites.

Video Production

I take raw footage and transform it into a polished, compelling story. My work includes editing, color grading, and adding motion graphics.

Prototyping & Fabrication

Ideas need to be tested. I create physical prototypes from my digital designs, using 3D printing to quickly test, refine, and perfect the final product.

About Me

I'm Szymon Hruszka, a passionate designer with a keen eye for detail and a drive for innovation. My work is a fusion of art and engineering, where I strive to create products that not only look stunning but also provide meaningful experiences.

With a background in both industrial design and 3D visualization, I possess a comprehensive skill set that covers the entire design process. I thrive on complex challenges and am dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in design and technology.
